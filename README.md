$$
\\frac{{V_a - V_1(s)}}{R} + \frac{{V_a - V_2(s)}}{\frac{1}{sC}} = 0\
$$

여기서 

$$
V_a = V_b = 0\
$$

이므로

$$
\-\frac{V_{1}(s)}{R}  -\frac{V_{2}(s)}{\frac{1}{sC}} = 0\
$$

정리하면  

$$
\(\frac{V_{2}(s)}{V_{1}(s)} = -\frac{1}{RCs}\)
$$
